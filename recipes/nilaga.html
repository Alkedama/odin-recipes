Beef Nilaga according to ChatGPT:

Beef nilaga is a comforting and hearty Filipino soup that's perfect for cold days or when you're craving a nourishing meal. Enjoy!

Ingredients:
  • 500 grams beef (preferably stewing beef), cut into cubes
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium-sized cabbage, chopped into large pieces
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (or to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water
  • Optional: whole black peppercorns, bay leaves
Steps:
  1. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic, and saut√© until the onions become translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  2. Add the beef cubes to the pot and cook until they brown slightly on all sides. This helps enhance the flavor of the dish.
  3. Pour enough water into the pot to cover the beef. If desired, you can also add a few whole black peppercorns and bay leaves for added flavor. Bring the water to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let the beef simmer for about 1 to 2 hours, or until it becomes tender. Skim off any impurities or foam that rise to the surface during simmering.
  5. Once the beef is tender, add the potatoes to the pot and continue simmering until they are cooked through and soft.
  6. Next, add the cabbage to the pot and let it cook for about 5 minutes or until it becomes tender but still slightly crisp. Adding the cabbage towards the end helps retain its freshness and texture.
  7. Season the dish with fish sauce, salt, and pepper according to your taste preferences. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  8. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes to let the flavors meld together. Check the seasoning one final time and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Remove the pot from the heat and serve the beef nilaga hot. It is commonly enjoyed with steamed rice.
